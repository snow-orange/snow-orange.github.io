Exploring the absolute wonder of Hungary

As I was wondering down the colourful street of Arany Janos in a daydream, the delicious smell of Hungary’s goulash paralysed me. The secret spice seasoning smoothly spread flowed out of the masterpiece and through my senses instantly summoning me in an unbreakable force. The town of Szentkiralyszabadja, and Ensana Grand Margaret Island hotel. I am going to tell you what it was like to meet these places when exploring the absolute wonder of Hungary.

On the first day I decided to fly out of the streets of Arany Janos and land in Szentkiralyszabadja: the haunted town. Szentkiralyszabadja is the most haunted place in Hungary, temperature at 13°C giving the whole place a look of a home for paranormal activity. I soon later found out that this creepy but fascinating buildings were built for the soldiers in 1960 to 1989. As I stepped out of the plane the cold instantly froze me as I felt my veins turn into ice, I could see a building all deserted, I smelled waste in the building, and it was a horrible smell of a combination of plastic, dust, and overgrown grass. The town, Szentkiralyszabadja, has been left abandoned for more than 2 years, all the towns walls are peeled and cracked, obviously no one wanting to even step foot in the land of Szentkiralyszabadja and fix them.

After the thrilling adventure of Szentkiralyszabadja, we can have a tremendous rest at a wondrous, colourful, busy restaurant called Retro goulash & langosh bar where the most famous food in Hungary is served: Goulash. It is a stew with meat that explodes with flavour when bit in, soft potato full of flavour that when you look at them you can almost taste the flavour and flaming seasoning balanced right between not-so-spicy and drinking-the-whole-pacific-ocean-spicy.

After a long day of exploring, I was exhausted, so I booked a grand hotel: Ensana grand margaret island. This hotel is the grandest hotel you can find in Hungary. With an indoor pool with a magnificent painting and a view of the outside nature, food put in front of you to pick from, and amazing service for you, with this, you cannot disagree with me.

Later I left Hungary after I had the biggest experience of my life, with only one conclusion left to express, Hungary is the best holiday destination on this planet to ever exist. To the warm welcoming smell of Hungary’s Goulash which summon people, to the cold deserted sense of Szentkiralyszabadja which people are too scared to enter: Hungary is absolutely, undenying, totally the most breath-taking place of them all of them.

Ethan Zhang
